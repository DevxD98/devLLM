# `notes/` — thinking out loud

## Why this folder exists

Not every thought is ready to be a doc, an ADR, or an experiment. `notes/` is the
low-friction scratch space for half-formed ideas, confusions, and "wait, why does this
work?" moments — kept in the repo so they aren't lost, and so a confusion resolved today
can be explained clearly tomorrow.

## What belongs here

- Rough derivations before they graduate to `docs/04_MATHEMATICS.md`
- "Things I got wrong and why" — misconceptions are teaching material
- Questions to answer later
- Links and quotes worth keeping

## What does NOT belong here

- Anything load-bearing. Once a note becomes a decision, promote it to an ADR. Once it
  becomes teaching, promote it to a `docs/` file. Notes are a staging area, not a
  destination.

## Convention

Free-form. Date-prefix files if it helps: `2026-07-24_why-layernorm-before-attention.md`.
Honesty over polish here — this is the one folder allowed to be messy.
